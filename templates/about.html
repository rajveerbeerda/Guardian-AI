About Us

Guardian AI started as a project to provide a portal to individuals especially the student community of India, which provides inoformation about how safe a particular locality/neighborhood is. The project was nicknamed 'your friendly neighborhood ai' as it uses AI techniques on news reportings to calculate safety index of any particular location. The aim of the project is to ensure that people make an informed decision about where they are going to live.
